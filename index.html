Federal Student Aid Redesign

Case Study: Create a user-centered redesign of the website based on user feedback and research.

Introduction: Federal Student Aid

Federal Student Aid, a part of the U.S. Department of Education, is the largest provider of student financial aid in the nation. FSA is responsible for managing the student financial assistance programs authorized under Title IV of the Higher Education Act of 1965. These programs provide grant, work-study, and loan funds to students attending college or career school.

Research

After creating a protopersona, we conducted user testing to test navigation flow and identify pain points including accessibility challenges.

Problem Statement

Users currently experience multiple pain points when interacting with the Federal Student Aid Website.

Prototype

We created a user-centered redesign of the website based on user feedback and research.

Testing and Iteration

Our user testing showed us many pain points that the users were facing when navigating the website including clutter, unclear buttons, challenges navigating pages, overwhelmgin hero images, unclear links. We made iterations to the website as needed.
